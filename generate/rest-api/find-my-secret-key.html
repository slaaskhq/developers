Find my Widget Secret Key

Your Widget Secret Key will allow you to interact with the Slaask REST API. This Secret Key is unique for every single widget in your team.

  • To get your Widget Secret Key, go to your Slaask account and click on Integrations (you can also follow this link)

  • Behind the integrations short description, click on the Show your widget(s) secret keys link.

  • Here are your secret keys, just copy it and keep it private!. Everyone with this Secret Key can consult or manage your data through the Slaask REST API.
